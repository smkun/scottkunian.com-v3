About Scott Kunian

Welcome to my world of tech, games, and creative adventures!

👨‍💻 Career Journey

I'm a Senior Systems Engineer at ClearMotion, constantly exploring new systems, technologies, and expanding my expertise in IT infrastructure. Recently, I’ve been diving deep into MATLAB/MathWorks and the Nextiva phone system.

Before ClearMotion, I completed an intense 26-week Software Engineering Bootcamp at General Assembly, which ignited my passion for coding and problem-solving.

🎲 A Life Full of Games

Gaming has been a huge part of my life since 1977 when I played my first D&D session. I’m part of a Thursday night RPG group that’s been running since 1989, where I enjoy both DMing and playing in various campaigns like D&D, Shadowrun, and more.

I also own 6 3D printers (4 Prusa MK4s, 2 Elegoo Saturn 4 Ultras) which I use to create epic miniatures, terrain, and full encounters for my game groups and game store.

🛠️ Work Meets Play

I co-own Midgard Hobbies & Games in Derry, NH, and help with IT and logistics at my wife’s flower shop, Affairs to Remember Florist.
